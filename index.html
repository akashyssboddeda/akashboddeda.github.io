Hello, I am Akash Boddeda, a Graduate Engineer Trainee at General Motors (GM).
Personally I love working on evolving technologies of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning and developing a deep understanding in these fields. I am aimed at learning Control of distributed, autonomous systems and automobiles. Presently I am working on the technical and manufacturing aspects of Engine and Automobile control at Global Propulsion Systems, GM.
I pursued Bachelors of Technology degree in Mechanical Engineering as Major and Civil Engineering as Minor from Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Guwahati, India; working with Prof. Dibakar Bandopadhya and Prof. Karuna Kalita.
I also worked with Prof. Keekyoung Kim at Integrated Bio-Micro/Nanotechnology Laboratory, The University of British Columbia (UBC), Canada and with Prof. Da-Jeng Yao at Institute of Nanoengineering and Microsystems, National Tsing Hua University (NTHU), Taiwan.
